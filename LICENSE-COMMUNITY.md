## 5. TRANSITION TO COMMERCIAL

### 5.1 When to Upgrade

You MUST upgrade to Commercial License when:

1. **Revenue Threshold**
   - Annual company revenue exceeds $100,000

2. **Venture Funding**
   - You accept venture capital or private equity funding
   - Angel investors investing > $50,000
   - Any institutional funding

3. **Commercialization**
   - You monetize the product or service
   - You charge users for anything
   - You offer it as a commercial product

4. **Mature Startup**
   - More than 5 full-time employees
   - Generating commercial revenue
   - Operating for more than 2 years

### 5.2 Compliance Check

- Kolibri reserves right to verify Community License compliance
- Annual self-certification required for ongoing use
- False statements may result in license termination and penalties
- Transition can be retroactive if commercialization occurs

### 5.3 Upgrade Process

When you need to upgrade:

1. **Notify Kolibri**
   - Email: licensing@kolibri.io
   - Subject: "Community to Commercial Upgrade"

2. **Select Commercial Tier**
   - Startup ($10K/year) if revenue < $1M
   - Business ($50K/year) if revenue $1M-$10M
   - Enterprise ($250K/year) if revenue > $10M

3. **Execute Commercial Agreement**
   - Sign LICENSE-COMMERCIAL.md
   - Setup billing
   - Get API keys for enterprise features

4. **Migration Support**
   - We help migrate to commercial infrastructure
   - No disruption to your service
   - Dedicated migration engineer

**A:** If YOU don't generate revenue (ads, conversion, etc.), it may be acceptable Community use. Reach out to licensing@kolibri.io for clarification.

**A:** Only with Community License (for non-commercial) or Commercial License (for commercial). If you modify core Kolibri, you must use AGPL-3.0 which requires opening source.

### Q: What if I go over 20 GB limit?

**A:** Contact us. We may upgrade you to Community+ ($500/year) or Commercial License.

### Q: Can I use Community License for "internal" company use?

**A:** Only if:
- Non-commercial (not serving customers)
- Company revenue < $100K/year
- Personal/internal tool only

### Q: What if I get acquired?

**A:** Acquirer must upgrade to Commercial License. Retroactive fees may apply.

### Q: When do I need to upgrade?

**A:** As soon as:
- Revenue exceeds $100K/year

### Q: Can I use Community License for evaluation?
